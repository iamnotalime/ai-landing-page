06 / Guardrails

AI Should Be Useful, Auditable, and Controlled

We do not build uncontrolled AI systems. Every serious workflow should be human-in-the-loop, auditable, permissioned, monitored, reversible, limited in scope, and designed around real business risk.

guardrail.check
  • pass Human approval before external action
  • pass Audit logs and evidence trail
  • pass Permissioned data access
  • pass Fallback flow for uncertainty
  • pass Monitoring dashboard and eval tests
  • hold Risky outputs routed to people
